🐾 Getting Started 6 questions
What is Sunday? ▾

Sunday is the dog lifestyle app for Singapore dog owners. It helps you discover verified dog-friendly places, track your dog's health records, and keep tabs on everything you spend on your pup — all in one place.

Sunday was built by a Singapore dog owner who spent years managing her dog's complex medical needs with no good tools. Every feature exists because she needed it.

Is Sunday free? ▾

Yes — Sunday's core features are free. You can browse all dog-friendly places, track your dog's health, log spending manually, and save up to 5 collections at no cost.

Sunday Plus (S$8.99/month or S$79.99/year) unlocks AI-powered features: unlimited Pin It, receipt scanning, health document uploads, and more Ask Sunday sessions.

Sunday Pro (S$15.99/month or S$149.99/year) is for power users who want unlimited Ask Sunday, priority support, and early access to new features.

How do I create an account? ▾

Tap Get Started on the welcome screen. You can sign up with Apple, Google, or email and password.

If you're on iPhone, we recommend Sign in with Apple — it's the fastest and most private option.

How do I add my dog? ▾

During onboarding you'll be asked to set up your dog's profile — name, breed, and date of birth. You can skip this and add your dog later from the My Pets tab.

Your dog's profile is the heart of Sunday — it connects their health records, vaccination schedule, medications, and spending all in one place.

Can I add more than one dog? Plus ▾

Free accounts support one dog profile. Sunday Plus lets you add multiple dogs — each with their own health records, spending history, and profile.

What's the difference between Plus and Pro? ▾

Sunday Plus (S$8.99/month) unlocks all AI features: unlimited Pin It, receipt scanning, health document uploads, 3 Ask Sunday sessions per month, and multiple dog profiles.

Sunday Pro (S$15.99/month) adds unlimited Ask Sunday sessions, priority support, and early access to new features — built for dog owners who use Sunday every day.

Both are billed through Apple in-app purchase. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

🧭 Explore & Places 7 questions
How are places verified? ▾

Every Sunday Verified place has been checked by our team. We confirm the dog policy, opening hours, amenities (shade, water bowls, indoor seating), and contact details before adding the verified badge.

Community-pinned places appear on the map too — these are added by Sunday users and Scouts, and reviewed periodically by our team.

What is the Sunday Score? ▾

The Sunday Score reflects how dog-friendly a place really is — not just whether dogs are allowed, but whether it's genuinely a good experience. It's based on community visits and ratings.

The more people who visit and rate a place, the more accurate the score becomes. You'll see it displayed as a number out of 100 on each place card.

How do I find places near me? ▾

Sunday uses your location (with your permission) to show the nearest places first. You'll see the distance from you on each place card.

Switch to the Map tab to see everything on a visual map and explore by area. Filter by category — Cafés, Vets, Parks, Malls, Stays, Grooming — or by price and Scout picks.

How do I save a place to my collection? ▾

Open any place and tap the save button. You'll be asked to add it to an existing collection (e.g. Cafés, Parks, Wishlist) or create a new one.

Free accounts can have up to 5 collections. Sunday Plus gives you unlimited collections.

A place's information has changed — how do I report it? ▾

On any place detail page, tap "Something changed?". You can tell us what's different — hours, dog policy, closed down, or anything else.

Our team reviews all reports and updates listings accordingly. We really appreciate these — it keeps Sunday accurate for everyone.

Can I add a place that's not on Sunday? ▾

Yes — tap the 📌 Pin It button on the Explore or Map screen. Paste a link from Instagram or TikTok and Sunday's AI will read the post and fill in the place details for you.

You can also add a place manually by filling in the details yourself.

What are Sunday's Dog Notes? ▾

Dog Notes are short, dog-specific summaries on each place — things like shade coverage, floor surfaces, whether there's space to roam, and how busy it gets.

They're written to help you decide if a place suits your specific dog, not just whether dogs are technically allowed.

📌 Pin It 5 questions
What is Pin It? ▾

Pin It lets you save any dog-friendly place you discover on Instagram or TikTok directly to Sunday's map. Copy the post link, tap 📌 Pin It, paste it in — Sunday's AI reads the post and fills in all the details automatically.

No more screenshot folders. Your finds live on the map where you can actually use them.

How many places can I pin? ▾

Free accounts get 5 AI-assisted Pin Its per month. Your counter resets at the start of each calendar month. You can always add places manually (no limit).

Upgrade to Sunday Plus for unlimited AI Pin Its.

Why can't Sunday read my post? ▾

Pin It works best with public Instagram and TikTok posts. It may not work if:

  • The account is private
  • The post is a story (not a permanent post)
  • The post doesn't mention a specific place
  • The link has expired or been removed

If it can't read the post, you can still add the place manually by filling in the details yourself.

What happens after I pin a place? ▾

Your pin appears on Sunday's map immediately as a community pin. Other Sunday users can see it too.

You'll also be prompted to rate the place if you've visited. Community ratings build the Sunday Score over time.

Can I edit a place I pinned? ▾

Yes — open the place and tap the edit option to update any details. If you spot something wrong with a place someone else pinned, use "Something changed?" to flag it for our team.

⭐ Scouts 4 questions
What is a Scout? ▾

Scouts are trusted Sunday community members verified by our team. Their pins and reviews are attributed on the map so you know exactly who recommended a place.

Scouts are active dog owners who explore Singapore regularly and share genuinely useful, first-hand recommendations — not reposts.

How do I become a Scout? ▾

Scouts are selected by the Sunday team. We look for active community members who regularly contribute quality pins and reviews.

There's no application form — if you're an active Sunday user, keep pinning and reviewing and you may be invited. We'll reach out directly.

How do I browse Scout picks? ▾

On the Explore screen, tap Sunday Scouts to browse picks from all our Scouts. Tap any Scout to see their profile and all their pins.

You can also filter Explore results by ⭐ Scouts to see only Scout-recommended places.

Can I filter a Scout's picks by category? ▾

Yes — on any Scout's profile page, you can filter their pins by category (Cafés, Parks, Vets, etc.) to find exactly what you're looking for.

🏥 My Pets & Health 7 questions
What's in My Pets? ▾

My Pets is your dog's complete dashboard. From here you can access:

  • Health Story — vet visits, conditions, health history
  • Vaccinations — upcoming and past vaccines with reminders
  • Medications — active meds with dose tracking
  • Spending — lifetime costs and monthly breakdown
  • Symptom Logger — quick symptom notes for vet visits
  • Health Records — full archive of all entries
How do I add a health record? ▾

Go to My Pets → Health Story and tap the add button. Fill in the date, type of entry (vet visit, condition, procedure), and any notes.

With Sunday Plus, you can also upload vet documents (prescriptions, test results, discharge summaries) directly to a health entry.

How do I log a symptom quickly? ▾

Go to My Pets → Symptom Logger and tap to add. Describe what you're seeing — vomiting, lethargy, changes in appetite — and save.

Every entry is automatically timestamped so you have an accurate record to show your vet. No need to enter the time manually — just tap and save as it happens.

How do I track vaccinations? ▾

Go to My Pets → Vaccinations and tap Add. Enter the vaccine name, date given, clinic, and due date for the next dose.

Sunday will remind you 14 days and 7 days before a vaccination is due so nothing slips through the cracks.

How do I track medications? ▾

Go to My Pets → Medications and tap Add. Enter the medication name, dosage, frequency, and time of dose. You can set a refill reminder so you never run out.

Past medications are stored too, giving you a complete medication history to share with any vet.

Can I upload vet documents? Plus ▾

Yes — with Sunday Plus you can upload vet records, prescriptions, test results, and discharge summaries directly to a health entry. Supported: photos (JPG, PNG) and PDFs.

All documents are private and only accessible by you. They are never shared.

Is my dog's health data private? ▾

Completely. Your dog's health records, uploaded documents, and symptom logs are private to your account only. We never share, sell, or analyse your pet's health data.

The only exception: when you use Ask Sunday, your dog's profile and health history are sent to our AI to generate a relevant response. This is disclosed clearly — see our Privacy Policy for full details.

🤖 Ask Sunday 5 questions
What is Ask Sunday? ▾

Ask Sunday is your AI companion that knows your dog. It reads your dog's profile, health history, vaccinations, and medications — and gives you answers that are actually relevant to your specific dog.

It's available as its own tab in the app — tap Ask Sunday at the bottom of the screen anytime.

What can I ask it? ▾

Ask Sunday is best for:

  • Triage — "Sunday vomited twice this morning, should I go to the vet?" It gives you a clear urgency signal: monitor at home, call your vet, or go to emergency now
  • Health questions — "What are the side effects of Optimmune?" "Is this vaccination overdue?"
  • Your dog's schedule — "When is Sunday's next vaccination?" "What medications is she on right now?"
  • Place recommendations — "Dog-friendly café near Holland V with shade"
Is Ask Sunday a replacement for my vet? ▾
No. Ask Sunday is a triage helper, not a veterinarian. It helps you decide how urgently to act — not what to do medically.

Every response includes a reminder that it is not a substitute for veterinary advice. For any real health concern, always consult a licensed vet. In an emergency, go directly to your nearest emergency animal clinic.

How many sessions do I get? ▾

Free — 1 Ask Sunday session per month

Sunday Plus — 3 sessions per month

Sunday Pro — unlimited sessions

Your session count resets at the start of each calendar month.

Does Ask Sunday share my dog's data? ▾

When you use Ask Sunday, your dog's profile and relevant health history are sent to our AI provider (OpenAI) to generate your response. This data is not retained beyond the immediate request under the API agreement we operate under.

We are fully transparent about this — full details are in our Privacy Policy.

💰 Spending Tracker 4 questions
How do I track my spending? ▾

Go to My Pets → Spending and tap Add entry. Choose the category, enter the amount, date, and any notes.

Categories include: Vet & Health, Food & Treats, Grooming, Accessories, Insurance, Boarding & Daycare, Transport, Training, Travel, and Other.

How does the receipt scanner work? Plus ▾

Tap the scan button on the Spending screen and point your camera at any receipt. Sunday reads it instantly and fills in the items, amounts, and category automatically.

Review the details and tap Confirm to save. If anything looks wrong, you can edit before saving.

Where can I see my total spending? ▾

The Spending screen shows your lifetime total at the top, with a breakdown by category. You can see exactly where your dog budget goes — vet bills, food, grooming, and more.

Can Sunday see my bank account? ▾

No. Sunday never connects to your bank account or payment cards. Spending is tracked only by what you manually log or scan via receipt.

Receipt images are processed to extract amounts and categories — the raw image is not stored after processing.

🦴 Treatos 4 questions
What is a Treato? ▾

A Treato is a voluntary S$9 contribution to support Sunday. Think of it as buying Sunday (the dog) a treat — a small thank-you from the community that keeps the app going.

It's not a subscription. You give one when you want to, as many times as you like. No recurring charges, ever.

What do I get for giving a Treato? ▾

Giving a Treato earns you:

  • 🐾 Treato badge on your profile — shown on all your pins and reviews permanently
  • ✅ Sunday Curator badge — your votes carry extra weight and you show as "Trusted Curator"
  • ⚡ Early access to new features before everyone else
  • 💌 A personal thank-you note from Kristin, written to you and your dog by name
Is a Treato a subscription? ▾

No. A Treato is a one-time voluntary payment. You will never be charged automatically.

Give as many as you like — each one adds to your total on the Supporters Wall.

How is a Treato different from Sunday Plus? ▾

Sunday Plus/Pro are subscriptions that unlock features. Treatos are voluntary community support — a one-time S$9 gift that earns you a permanent community badge.

They are completely separate. You can have Plus without Treatos, Treatos without Plus, or both.

🛒 Curate 3 questions
What is Curate? ▾

Curate is a hand-picked selection of products that Sunday (the dog) actually uses. Every item has been tried and tested — nothing is listed because it's popular or because someone paid to be there.

It's a shortcut for Singapore dog owners who want trusted product recommendations without wading through reviews.

Are Curate links affiliate links? ▾

Some are. When you tap "Get it for Sunday →", some links earn Sunday a small commission from the retailer — at no extra cost to you.

This is disclosed clearly on the Curate screen. It never influences what gets listed — a product is only in Curate if it's genuinely used and recommended.

Can I suggest a product for Curate? ▾

Yes — email us at hello@sundaytheapp.com and suggest a product. We'll look into it and try it ourselves before adding it.

We only add what we'd genuinely give to our own dog.

👤 Account & Privacy 5 questions
How do I change my password? ▾

Go to Profile → Change Password. Enter your current password and choose a new one.

If you signed up with Apple or Google, your password is managed through those accounts — you won't see a Change Password option.

How do I control my notifications? ▾

Go to Profile → Notifications. You can toggle:

  • Vaccination reminders (14 days and 7 days before due)
  • Medication reminders
  • Sunday app announcements

You can also manage notification permissions in iPhone Settings → Sunday.

How do I delete my account? ▾

Go to Profile → Delete Account. Read the warning carefully — deleting your account permanently removes all your health records, spending history, pins, collections, and profile.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. All your data is removed from our systems at the moment you confirm. This action cannot be undone.

What data does Sunday collect? ▾

Sunday collects what you give it — your profile, dog details, health entries, spending records, and any pins or reviews you submit. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose outside of running Sunday.

Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel Sunday Plus or Pro? ▾

Sunday subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel:

  • Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions
  • Find Sunday and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. Sunday cannot process refunds — contact Apple directly for billing disputes.

🔧 Troubleshooting 5 questions
The map isn't loading. What do I do? ▾

The map requires an internet connection. Check that you're connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data.

If you're connected but the map still won't load: close the app completely and reopen it. If it's still not working, let us know at support@sundaytheapp.com.

Pin It isn't reading my post. ▾

A few things to check:

  • Make sure the post is public (not from a private account)
  • Use a permanent post link, not a story or reel
  • Copy the full URL from the browser, not just the short link
  • Try again — AI features occasionally have brief delays

If none of these help, you can always add the place manually after tapping Pin It.

The receipt scanner didn't read my receipt correctly. ▾

For best results:

  • Make sure the receipt is flat and fully visible in the frame
  • Good lighting makes a big difference — avoid shadows
  • Hold the camera steady for a moment before capturing

If the scan comes out wrong, you can edit the details before saving, or discard and add manually instead.

I can't sign in to my account. ▾

If you've forgotten your password, tap Forgot Password? on the sign-in screen and we'll send a reset link to your email.

If you signed in with Apple or Google, make sure you're using the same method you used when you first signed up — you can't mix them.

Still stuck? Email us at support@sundaytheapp.com and we'll sort it out.

Something in the app looks wrong or broken. How do I report it? ▾

We want to know. Email support@sundaytheapp.com and describe what you're seeing. Screenshots are very helpful.

Sunday is built by a small team and your reports directly shape what gets fixed next. We read every message.

Still need help?

Email us at support@sundaytheapp.com and we'll get back to you.
