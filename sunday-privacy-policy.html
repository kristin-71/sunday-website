1. Who We Are

Sunday is a dog lifestyle app for Singapore dog owners — built to help you find dog-friendly places, track your dog's health, and keep every memory in one place.

Sunday is operated by a sole proprietorship based in Singapore. Sunday is not affiliated with any veterinary clinic, pet insurance provider, or pet brand.

Contact: hello@sundaytheapp.com

2. What We Collect and Why

Your account

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. This is the minimum needed to identify your account, let you sign in, and send you essential notifications.

Your dog's profile Sensitive

When you add a dog, you provide details about them: name, breed, date of birth, weight, microchip number, and photo. This information is used only to personalise your experience and power features like Ask Sunday. It is never shared with third parties for any purpose other than what is described in this policy.

Health records Sensitive

Sunday stores your dog's:

  • Vaccination history (vaccine name, date given, due date, clinic)
  • Medications (name, dosage, frequency, schedule, refill dates)
  • Vet visits and health entries (type, date, notes, care tips)
  • Symptom logs
  • Health documents you upload (Plus tier)

This data is stored securely in your account. It is never shared with vets, insurers, pet brands, or any other party. It is shared with OpenAI only when you actively use Ask Sunday — see Section 3.

Explicit consent required. Before Sunday collects your dog's health data, we ask for your explicit consent in-app. You can withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account.

Saved places and collections

Places you save, collections you create, Pin It saves, and community votes are stored in your account. If you submit a place as a Scout, your display name may appear alongside your contribution if you choose to be credited — you can opt out at the time of submission.

Spending records

Spending entries and receipt photos you upload are stored in your account. Receipt images are sent to Google Gemini to extract spending data. Images are not retained by Google beyond the immediate request.

3. AI Providers — What Goes Where

Sunday uses two AI providers. We are transparent about what is sent to each.

Google Gemini

Used for: Pin It (extracting place details from social media posts), Receipt Scanner (reading spending receipts), and generating dog-friendly notes for places.

What is sent: social media captions, receipt images, place descriptions. No personal account data or health records.

OpenAI (GPT)

Used for: Ask Sunday — the in-app AI companion.

What is sent: your dog's profile (name, breed, age, weight) and relevant health history (vet visits, vaccinations, medications, symptom logs) to generate a personalised, contextual response.

Important: When you use Ask Sunday, your dog's health information is sent to OpenAI's API to generate your answer. OpenAI does not retain this data beyond the immediate API request under standard enterprise API terms. Ask Sunday is a triage helper only — it is not a substitute for veterinary advice. Any health concern should be confirmed with a licensed vet.

Neither Google nor OpenAI use your data to train their models under the API agreements Sunday operates under.

4. Location Data

Sunday requests access to your location only when you use the Map feature. When you open the Map, the app prompts you for explicit permission. Location is used in real time to show nearby dog-friendly places — it is not stored on our servers.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings. The app continues to work without location — you simply won't see distance-based results.

5. Usage Analytics

Sunday uses PostHog to understand how people use the app — which screens are visited, which features are used most, how long sessions last, and where users drop off. This helps us improve the app and decide what to build next.

PostHog data is anonymised and cannot be linked back to you personally. We do not track individual users across sessions for advertising purposes. No health data or dog profile data is included in analytics events.

6. Notifications

Sunday sends push notifications for:

  • Vaccination reminders (14 days and 7 days before a due date)
  • Medication refill reminders
  • Important account or app announcements

We do not send promotional or marketing notifications without your separate consent. You can manage notification preferences in your device Settings at any time.

7. Payments

Sunday Plus and Sunday Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple In-App Purchase. Sunday never sees, handles, or stores your payment card details — all billing is managed directly by Apple.

For support with billing, contact Apple directly via your Apple ID account settings.

9. Data Storage and Security

All Sunday data is stored on Supabase servers in Singapore (ap-southeast-1 region). We have implemented the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • All data is encrypted at rest
  • Row-level security (RLS) is enforced — you can only ever access your own data
  • Authentication is required for all data operations

Sunday is built with Singapore's PDPA and GDPR principles in mind from the start — not added later.

10. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, all of the following is permanently and immediately deleted:

  • Your account and profile
  • All dog profiles and health records
  • Vaccinations, medications, and vet visits
  • Spending entries and receipt images
  • Saved places and collections
  • Ask Sunday conversation history

Anonymous analytics events (not linkable to you) are retained for product improvement purposes.

To delete your account: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

11. Your Rights

Sunday is built to meet Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and is aligned with GDPR principles for international users. You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data Sunday holds about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for us to collect, use, or disclose your data
  • Deletion — request immediate deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

Email hello@sundaytheapp.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 10 business days.

12. Children

Sunday is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through Sunday, contact us immediately at hello@sundaytheapp.com and we will delete it.

13. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you via in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of Sunday after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

The current version is always available at sundaytheapp.com/privacy.
