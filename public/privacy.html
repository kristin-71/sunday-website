1. Who We Are

Sunday is a pet lifestyle app for pawrents in Singapore — built to help you find pet-friendly places, track your pet's health, and keep every memory in one place.

Sunday is operated in Singapore and is not affiliated with any veterinary clinic, pet insurance provider, or pet brand.

Data Protection Officer: privacy@sundaytheapp.com

2. What We Collect and Why

Your account

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. This is the minimum needed to identify your account, let you sign in, and send you essential notifications.

Your pet's profile Sensitive

When you add a pet, you provide details like name, breed, date of birth, weight, microchip number, and photo. This information is used only to personalise your experience and is never shared with third parties for marketing or advertising.

Health and wellness records Sensitive

Sunday stores your pet's health and wellness information, including but not limited to:

  • Vaccination history
  • Medications and routines
  • Vet visits and health entries
  • Symptom logs and vitals
  • Grooming records

This data is stored securely in your account. It is never shared with vets, insurers, or pet brands. It is shared with Google Gemini only when you actively use Ask Sunday — see Section 3.

Explicit consent required. Before your pet's health data is shared with any AI service, you are asked for explicit consent in-app. You can withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.

Saved places and collections

Places you save, collections you create, and community contributions are stored in your account. If you submit a place as a Scout, your display name may appear alongside your contribution — you can opt out at the time of submission.

Spending records

Spending entries and receipt images you upload are stored in your account. Receipt images are processed by Google Gemini to extract spending data — images are not retained by Google beyond the immediate request.

3. How We Use AI

Sunday uses AI to power several features. Here is exactly what is sent and to whom.

Google Gemini

Used for: Ask Sunday (AI health and lifestyle companion), Receipt Scanner (reading spending receipts), and generating place notes.

What is sent to Ask Sunday: your question, your pet's profile (name, breed, age, weight), and relevant health history (vet visits, vaccinations, medications, symptom logs) to generate a personalised response.

What is sent for Receipt Scanner: receipt images. No personal account data or health records.

OpenAI

Used for: Pin It (extracting place details from social media posts you share with the app).

What is sent: social media captions and page metadata. No personal account data, pet data, or health records.

Important: Neither Google nor OpenAI retain your data beyond the immediate API request. Neither provider uses your data to train their models. Ask Sunday is a wellness companion — it is not a substitute for veterinary advice. Any health concern should be confirmed with a licensed vet.

4. Shared Access (Co-Pawrents)

Sunday lets you invite others to share access to your pet's profile — for example, a partner, family member, or pet sitter.

  • When you invite a co-pawrent, we send an email invitation to the address you provide
  • By inviting someone, you confirm you have their permission to share their email with Sunday
  • Co-pawrents can view and add to shared pet profiles, including health records
  • You can revoke a co-pawrent's access at any time

5. Location Data

Sunday requests location access only when you use the Map or Explore features. Location is used in real time to show nearby pet-friendly places — it is not stored on our servers.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings. The app works without location — you simply won't see distance-based results.

6. Usage Analytics

Sunday uses PostHog to understand how people use the app — which screens are visited, which features are used most, and where the experience can improve.

Analytics data is anonymised and cannot be linked back to you personally. No health data or pet profile data is included in analytics events.

7. Notifications

Sunday sends push notifications for:

  • Vaccination and medication reminders
  • Important account updates

We do not send promotional or marketing notifications without your separate consent. You can manage notification preferences in your device Settings.

8. Payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple In-App Purchase. Sunday never sees, handles, or stores your payment details — all billing is managed by Apple.

10. Where Your Data Lives

Your account and pet data is stored on Supabase servers in Singapore (ap-southeast-1 region).

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
  • Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data
  • Authentication is required for all data operations

11. How Long We Keep Your Data

We keep your data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, all of the following is permanently and immediately deleted:

  • Your account and profile
  • All pet profiles and health records
  • Vaccinations, medications, and vet visits
  • Spending entries and receipt images
  • Saved places and collections
  • Ask Sunday conversation history

Anonymous analytics events (not linkable to you) are retained for product improvement.

To delete your account: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

12. Your Rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and aligned with GDPR for international users, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — request immediate deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Withdrawal of consent — withdraw consent for us to collect, use, or disclose your data
  • Object — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

Email privacy@sundaytheapp.com with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We will respond within 10 business days.

13. Children

Sunday is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through Sunday, contact us at privacy@sundaytheapp.com and we will delete it immediately.

14. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights, we will notify affected users and the relevant authorities within 3 calendar days of confirming the breach, in accordance with Singapore's PDPA.

15. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify you via in-app notification at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of Sunday after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

The current version is always available at sundaytheapp.com/privacy.
